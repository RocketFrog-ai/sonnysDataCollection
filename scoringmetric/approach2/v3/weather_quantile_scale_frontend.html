Approach 1

Midpoint of min & max wash

For each side of your value, the stat tile shows (min wash + max wash) / 2 among peers below or above your metric.

Approach 2

Average wash + site count

For each side, the stat tile shows the mean annual wash across all peers below or above your metric, plus how many sites are in each group.

Recommended

Median wash + site count

Median is outlier-resistant — one mega-site can't skew the number. This section shows median annual wash for peers below and above your value, with group sizes.
